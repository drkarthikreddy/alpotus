Quick Syntax Guide:

Direction: `graph TD` (Top to Bottom), `graph LR` (Left to Right)

Nodes (Shapes):

A["Rectangle with text"] B("Rounded text") C(("Circle text")) D{"Diamond text"} E>"Asymmetric text"] F{{"Hexagon text"}}

Links (Arrows):

A --> B; (Arrow head) A --- B; (No head) A -- "text" --> B; (With text, use quotes) A -.- B; (Dotted line) A ==> B; (Thick line)
